About World Wide Inu

$WWI — World Wide Inu has one simple goal. To put up a flag in every continent, country and ultimately city of the world. A flag that represents unity and commitment from our community, we want to put up our flag and let the world know that WWI is in this together:

▶ 7 Continents
▶ 195 Countries
▶ 4,037 Cities (Population greater than 100,000)

Whats the plan?

To achieve our goal we have set up a simple road map to get us in track. Sharing the idea and acceptance from the crypto space.

Deployment of ERC-20 contract

    5% transaction tax — $WWI Flag operation funding ( Purchase and shipping of flags to community members around the world, sponsor travel budgets to certain cities and countries where we have no access to and other project costs)


Build a community on Telegram and Discord channels

    As this project will take a good amount of participation from community members we will need a very effective manner of communication and organize information properly for proper execution and success of our goal.

Flag Production

Our very first step towards literally marking History throughout the world. We have found a couple of flag production companies that will happily take our order in an expedited manner. We will begin by producing 7 flags, 1 for each continent and then we will go ahead to order the next batch of ~200 flags for each country and so on until our journey has been completed.

Flag it up!

The mission has begun, we are on the road to flag up our next spot. Put up the flag, snap a picture and send it to our community channels for review. Once accepted and registered, we will manually mint a NFT with that image and airdrop it back to the original creator. Only way to gain a NFT is by requesting a flag and physically going to X location and flag it up!

Documentation

We will be very creative in ways to showcase our work as a community. For easy management of data and giving creators credits where its due, we will be minting a NFT collection as mentioned above. Each token will have the exact location under its metadata and will be able to be seen via our Web3 Album through our website to be established during the first weeks of our launch.

This project is all about having fun with the community and leaving something that will last in the blockchain as well as in real-life. Im sure we will be very happy to see the album stored on-chain some years from now and remembering how crazy and fun was it to put up flags around the world. Has that ever been done?

Hope we can accomplish our goal and we are very excited to keep building and adding fuel to the fire if the community allows us to grow.

Happy Flagging!
